Features

Everything you need to run your venue.

Digital Agreements

Send Staff Uniform & Professional Conduct Agreements instantly. Capture digital signatures that are legally binding and stored securely.

SMS Onboarding Bot

Automate the onboarding conversation. Our AI bot collects basic info, tax docs, and photos via SMS, making it easy for staff to join.

Staffing Matrix

Calculate required headcount for any event based on guest count. Ensure you have the right ratio of servers, bartenders, and leads automatically.

SMS Time Tracking

Staff clock in and out via text message. Accurate timestamps, GPS verification, and automated break compliance monitoring built-in.

Tip Pool Management

Automate tip distributions and pool calculations. Set custom rates for different roles and ensure fair, transparent payouts every time.

Performance Insights

Track incident reports, shift swap reliability, and performance ratings. Build a data-driven view of your best team members.
