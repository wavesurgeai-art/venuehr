## Introduction
Our clients entrust us with one of the most significant days of their lives. As a member of the service team, you are an integral part of the event's atmosphere and success. This agreement outlines the professional standards required to maintain our "once-in-a-lifetime" experience.

## 1. The Brand Standard (Uniform & Appearance)
You are part of the decor. Your appearance must be impeccable at all times.

*   **The Look:** [e.g., Solid black button-down shirt, black dress slacks, and black non-slip dress shoes].
*   **Grooming:** All clothing must be professionally pressed, clean, and entirely free of lint or pet hair.
*   **Visible Items:** 
    *   No visible headphones or AirPods are permitted.
    *   Heavy fragrances (perfume/cologne) are prohibited.
    *   Excessive jewelry that may interfere with service or detract from the uniform standard is not allowed.

## 2. The "Invisible" Service Standard
The hallmark of excellent service is being present when needed and invisible when not.

*   **Cell Phone Policy:** Cell phones must be stored in staff lockers or your personal vehicle. Texting, social media use, or personal calls are strictly prohibited on the event floor.
*   **Guest Interaction:** Always yield the right of way to guests. If a guest asks a question you cannot answer, use the following response: *"I will find out for you immediately,"* then promptly alert the Lead Coordinator.
*   **Consumption:** No eating, drinking (except for water in designated staff areas), smoking, or vaping is permitted within view of guests.

## 3. Professional Boundaries
We maintain a strict boundary between staff and guests to ensure a professional environment.

*   **Non-Fraternization:** You are here to serve the wedding, not join it.
    *   Do not accept drinks (alcoholic or otherwise) from guests.
    *   Do not join the dance floor.
    *   Do not request photos with the wedding party or high-profile guests.
*   **Alcohol Service (Bartenders Only):** 
    *   Strict adherence to Indiana ATC guidelines is mandatory.
    *   Never "over-pour" for a guest.
    *   Staff consumption of alcohol during or after a shift on venue property is strictly prohibited.

## 4. Social Media, Privacy & Confidentiality
The privacy of our clients is paramount.

*   **Social Media:** Do not post photos or videos of the wedding party, their decor, or their guests to personal social media accounts without explicit written permission from the Venue Manager.
*   **Confidentiality:** Respect the privacy of our clients. What you hear or see at a private event stays at the event.
