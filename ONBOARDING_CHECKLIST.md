### Phase 1: Administrative & Compliance
- [ ] Employee information form completed
- [ ] Tax forms (W-4 / I-9) submitted and verified
- [ ] Direct deposit authorization submitted
- [ ] Emergency contact information provided
- [ ] Copy of valid photo ID on file

### Phase 2: Professional Standards & Training
- [ ] **Professional Conduct & Uniform Agreement reviewed and signed**
- [ ] Uniform standards explained (The "Brand Standard")
- [ ] Grooming and appearance requirements reviewed
- [ ] Cell phone and personal device policy explained
- [ ] Guest interaction protocol training ("The Invisible Standard")
- [ ] Privacy and Social Media policy reviewed

### Phase 3: Facility Orientation
- [ ] Venue tour (guest areas vs. staff areas)
- [ ] Staff locker and break area location
- [ ] Restroom locations (staff-specific)
- [ ] Designated smoking/vaping areas (if applicable, out of guest view)
- [ ] Emergency exits and first aid kit locations

### Phase 4: Role-Specific Training
- [ ] Job description review
- [ ] Role-specific uniform items issued (aprons, name tags, etc.)
- [ ] Equipment training (POS, coffee machines, etc.)
- [ ] **Bartenders Only:** Indiana ATC compliance review and certificate verification

### Phase 5: Final Review
- [ ] Key contact list provided (Manager, Lead Coordinator)
- [ ] Schedule for the first two weeks confirmed
- [ ] Questions and Answer session completed
